Welcome to Yunnan :)

NCrossing-the-bridge noodles is a rice noodle soup originating from the Yunnan province of China.
It is one of the best-known dishes in Yunnan cuisine.
The dish is served with a large bowl of boiling hot broth and soup.
The soup is made with chicken, pork bone and seasoning, such as Chinese star anise and ginger.
A layer of chicken fat is also used to insulate the soup and keep it warm for longer.
